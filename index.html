Interested in using ML models for application centric search of materials

Hello, my name is Vipul. I have hands-on research experience in conducting first-principles studies during my bachelor internship and bachelor thesis. This experience inspired me to pursue an MSc degree in the Elite Master’s program Advanced Materials and Processes (MAP) at Friedrich-Alexander University (FAU), Erlangen.

Since starting my studies at FAU, I have worked as a research assistant in several research groups in the university, which has provided me with valuable insight into both experimental and theoretical research workflows. Further, having worked at BASF SE, Ludwigshafen, I got an excellent opportunity to explore how research findings can be translated into real-world applications. Currently, I am working on finetuning the Automatic speech recognization (ASR) models using pytorch.
