Hello, I’m Vipul Kumar Ambasta. I am Chemical engineer who is fascinated by the world of Materials. Currently, I am pursuing towards my MSc. degree in Elite Master's degree 'Advanced Materials and Processes' (MAP) in Friedrich-Alexander University of Erlangen-Nuremberg (FAU), Germany. Having hands-on research experience with conducting first principle's study during my bachelor internship and bachelor thesis, I decided to pursue my MSc degree in Elite Master's program 'Advanced Materials and Processes' (MAP). Since, starting my studies at FAU, I have worked as research assistant in several groups in university, which gave me understanding about how the research (both experimental and theoretical) work in the university. Currently, I am working as research intern in BASF SE, Ludwigshafen, which is perfect opportunity for me to understand how the learnings from research find real life applications.
